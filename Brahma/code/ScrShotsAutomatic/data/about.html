FAQ's
Q: What does Brahma do?
A: Brahma will let you take screen shots of your favorite application in an automated manner. It facilitates taking single & multiple screenshots for a fixed duration. It also embeds your watermark.

Q: What all Symbian platforms are supported?
A: Brahma v2012.1 supports Symbian^3 devices. The S60 5th edition support is not tested, but in theory this application should also run on them.

Q: How to support future development of Brahma?
A: The many ways in which the future development of Brahma can be supported are:
  • The simplest and best way is to buy the app from Ovi store.
  • Get the source code from sourceforge, make your own copy and show it off to others.
  • Contribute ideas and improvements to future versions.
  • Review it on your site or blog.
  • Report pirated copies of this application. Yes, even though the source code is open, there will be still people who will the app binary sold through Ovi Store. So please report such occurances.
  • Report license violations.
  • Simply take screenshots using it and brag about it :).
  • If the above forms don't fit your bill, simply send a mail to brahma@flaminghorns.com for any queries, reports, bugs, appreciation or critical comments.
Q: What all applications can be launched in the "App screenshots till exit" mode?
A: Currently only Symbian C++ native UI apps are supported. Widgets (.html) & midlets(.fakeapp) are not shown in the app list and hence need to be manually launched.
